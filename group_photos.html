Let's enjoy the life!

Shanghai Friends

Locals, Expats, or Visitors; Mandarin and/or English

A group dedicated to those times when you just need to chill out with some cool buddies.

Special food · Shanghai dialect · kung fu & table tennis · Language & Culture · Make New Friends · Meeting New People · Handmade Crafts · Volunteer · Kids

This is a social group where you get to meet people from all walks of life, and make new friends, in a safe, free, and enjoyable environment. We'll do language exchange, socialize in a coffee shop, or hang out for a fun night out. We may have game nights, movie nights, potlucks, beers on the couch or frisbee in the park. If a crazy occasion presents itself we may hit up a party, concert, dance club...

Everyone's welcome!

Let's meet up! You never try, you never know how happy we are:) Just do it and dream BIG!!
